About Us

A trusted name in hardware and industrial supply.

Uy Tit & Company, Inc. is a prominent and established distributor of hardware tools, power tools, and a wide range of industrial products in the Philippines.

With a rich history dating back to 1868, the company has consistently provided quality products and dependable service to various industries, making it a trusted name in the market.

Uy Tit & Company takes pride in its commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and contributing to the growth of businesses across the country.
